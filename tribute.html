Engr. Antonio Marzan

The Engineer who build not only houses but also a strong Family and Nation.

Here's a time line of Engr. Marzan's life:

  • 1937 - Born in San Quintin, Pangasinan
  • 1955 - Went to manila to pursue his dreams in University Of Santo Tomas, due to lack of financial resources, he applied for scholarship to pursue his education.
  • 1961 - He finished his Bachelor Degree of Civil Engineering and passed the Board Exam at the same year.
  • 1962 - Worked in different companies and met her soon to be wife.
  • 1967 - Marries his wife at the age of 30 and had their first son, Armando(my father).
  • 1977 - He retired from being an Engineer and follows the path of his father to become a politician, he started being Barangay Kagawad.
  • 1987 -Finished his 3 term of being Barangay Kagawad and decided to become the Barangay Captain, unfortunately he lost and losses his interest of being a politician.
  • 2000 - He then reclaim the interest in politics and run once again as Barangay Captain, and finally he won.
  • 2010 - He finished his term and decided to live with his family instead, cherish every moment he has. He ventured and traveled with his family.
  • 2013 - He was diagnosed with a mild stroke and affected his voice, he can't speak clearly and loudly.
  • 2014 - Died at the age of 77.
